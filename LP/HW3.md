### 2.2

Minimize: Cost = 25x + 20y
Subject to:
2x + y ≥ 12
2x + 9y ≥ 36
2x + 3y ≥ 24
x, y ≥ 0

1.  Form Augented Matrix for Inequalities
```
x y | b
----|---
2 1 | 12
2 9 | 36
2 3 | 24
----|---
25 20 | 0
```

2. *Transpose it*

```
u v w | b
------|---
2 2 2 | 25
1 9 3 | 20
------|---
12 36 24 | 0
```
3. Dual Maximization Problem (*New Objective Function and Constraints*):

Maximize: $Z = 12u + 36v + 24w$

Subject to (*now non-negative*):
$2u + 2v + 2w \leq -25$
$1u + 9v + 3w \leq 20$
$u, v , w \geq 0$

4. Perform Simplex Method
